
# Skateboarding Tutorial

## Getting Started

- Choose a flat, smooth surface like an empty parking lot or quiet sidewalk.
- Wear a helmet, wrist guards, and knee and elbow pads.
- Place your front foot near the front bolts and your back foot on the tail, with knees slightly bent.

## Basic Skills

- Practice pushing by keeping your front foot on the board and using your back foot to push off in short, controlled motions.
- Turn by leaning your body gently in the direction you want to go while keeping your weight centered.
- Stop by dragging your back foot lightly on the ground or shifting more weight to your back foot.

## Practice Tips

- Work on one skill at a time and repeat until it feels natural.
- Skate with friends or at a local skatepark to get feedback and encouragement.
- Keep a simple log of skills you are learning to track your progress.
